# Ashrafur Khan Portfolio

Welcome to my IST 110 digital portfolio. This site is hosted with GitHub Pages and organized to document my coursework throughout the semester. 

My name is Ashrafur Khan, and I am a Senior at Penn State majoring in Computational Data Science through the College of Engineering, with minors in Supply Chain, Information Sciences and Technology and Math. While I began my academic career undecided, I have since built a professional foundation through roles in accessibility research, software engineering, and data engineering.

My initial interest in computers started when I got my first laptop. It was an old hand-me-down Windows machine that barely worked and was quickly falling apart. What made it fun for me was trying to figure out the different ways that I could still get the computer to work so I could play whatever games that I was interested in at the time, and that didn't last very long because I installed a virus on it shortly thereafter. However, that wasn't the end of my time with that laptop. After wiping the drive clean and rebooting it, I learned the different ways I could keep it alive for quite a few more years to come. That is what really jumpstarted my interest in technology, and I attribute those experiences for why I chose to go into the field of computer science.
